2iBroker - Escobar Edition

Dashboard

The first row of the iBroker shows current Trading Data for BTC in Euro from Bitstamp Exchange.

The other rows of the iBroker show you the current prices in Euro.

Bitcoin

The first row of the iBroker shows current Trading Data for BTC in Euro from Bitstamp Exchange.

The other rows of the iBroker show you the current conversion factors for the respective asset.

Bitcoin, Ethereum, Monero, Litecoin, ..., Siacoin

The first row of the iBroker shows you the current exchange data from the Default Exchange in Preferences Menu.

The other rows of the iBroker show you the current conversion factors for the respective asset.

Colors

GREEN Asset with Gain
YELLOW Asset with exchange loss
MAGENTA Asset with 5% Gain or more
ORANGE Asset with 5% exchange loss or more
BLUE Asset with highest Gain
RED Asset with highest exchange loss

Statusbar of the iBroker

Investment-Rate Exchange-Rate Profit

A positive Investment-Rate says, that the current price is over the real price.

A negative Investment-Rate says, that the current price is under the real price.

Portfolio Management

Enter your portfolio into the respective fields of the assets Bitcoin, Ethereum, Monero, Litecoin, ..., Siacoin
IBroker calculates your total assets in euros based on your assets.

Buttons

The Home-Symbol opens the Standard-Webpage for the current tab - see preferences.
The Arrows will start trading webpages.
The Exchange-Button starts the Default-Wallet-Application for the current asset.
By clicking the Euro-Symbol a facts sheet appears - in theory.

If you bought

Clicking the Asset-Symbol updates the checkpoints for the current asset and BTC.


If you have sold

Clicking the Asset-Symbol updates the checkpoint for BTC.


In Common

Clicking the blue info icon updates the checkpoint for the current asset.

Additional Informations

The current Update-Intervall is 1 minute.
The Calculator will update too - be careful.

In /var/log/system.log are the charts for the last weeks.
In the console utility, set up a new system log request "Sender is equal to iBroker".
